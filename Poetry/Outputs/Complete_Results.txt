### Explanation:
The corrected verse in Spanish would be:

Crucé en vano, ignorante del camino,
a donde tú ya no estás.

Idiota y áspera mi existencia
sin ti, me despedí de mí mismo. 

[1] "Cruces" was changed to "Crucé", which is the correct conjugation of the verb cruzar (to cross) in past tense.
[2] The phrase "ásperamente integrado conocerme decirte adiós" was restructured and corrected as "ignorante del camino, a donde tú ya no estás." This maintains the sense of not knowing where
